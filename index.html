The App accesses the device camera solely to enable augmented reality and camera-based features provided by Snap Camera Kit.

The App does not require user accounts and does not knowingly collect personal identifying information such as name, email address, or phone number.

The App uses Snap Inc.’s Camera Kit SDK. Snap may collect and process data in accordance with their own privacy policy.

Data Storage
We do not store personal data on our servers.
